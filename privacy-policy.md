# Privacy Policy  
**No More Shorts**

_Last updated: 31 Jan 2026_

## Introduction
No More Shorts (“the App”) is designed to help users reduce distractions by preventing access to YouTube Shorts and Instagram Reels. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

---

## Information Collection and Use
**No More Shorts does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.**

- We do **not** collect personal information  
- We do **not** collect usage data  
- We do **not** track user behavior  
- We do **not** send any data to external servers  
- All functionality operates **entirely on the user’s device**

---

## Accessibility Service Usage
The App uses the **Android Accessibility Service** solely to detect when YouTube Shorts or Instagram Reels content is displayed on the screen and to prevent access by triggering a system navigation action (such as the back button).

### Important notes:
- Accessibility permission is **required for core functionality**  
- The service **does not read, collect, store, or transmit any screen content**  
- Accessibility data is **never logged, shared, or sent off-device**  
- The service is used strictly for **blocking Shorts/Reels and nothing else**

This usage complies with Google Play’s Accessibility policy.

---

## Battery Optimization Permission
The App may request permission to **exclude battery optimizations** to ensure uninterrupted operation while monitoring and blocking Shorts or Reels.

- This permission is used only to keep the app running reliably  
- No data is collected or transmitted as a result

---
